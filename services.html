Our services at WatchNow

At WatchNow, we offer a range of services to ensure that your watch stays in excellent condition and continues to keep accurate time. Our expert watchmakers and technicians have the experience and knowledge to handle all types of watches, from vintage timepieces to modern smartwatches. Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Battery change: If your watch is running low on power, bring it to us and we will replace the battery with a high-quality replacement.
  • Watch Strap Replacement: If your watch strap is worn or damaged, we can replace it with a new one that fits your watch perfectly.
  • Watch Servicing: Regular servicing of your watch is important to maintain its accuracy and prolong its lifespan. Our watchmakers can perform a full service on your watch, including cleaning, oiling, and regulating the movement.
  • Watch Repair: If your watch has stopped working or is damaged, bring it to us and we will detect the problem and provide a repair solution.
  • Water Resistance Testing: If your watch is designed to be water-resistant, we can test its water resistance to ensure that it is still working as intended.
  • Service Warranty: After a complete servicing of your watch, WatchNow offers a one year service warranty on all brand-name watches.
  • Our Recommendation: We reccommend that you have your watch checked every two years. If your watch is broken and stops working,you are in good hand at our services centers.

For more information on our services or to schedule an appointment, please contact us.
